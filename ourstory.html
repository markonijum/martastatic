Our Story

BelleTurban is a growing fashion brand that specializes in designing and manufacturing fashion and wellness turbans and scarves for women.

Our Mission is to break into the market with our innovative offer of products that create a strong and distinct fashion statements for our customers. We pride ourselves on our expertise and commitment to deliver exclusive, high quality and fashionable products.

Our Products are 100% designed and manufactured in Spain, fulfilling European technical regulations, offering the highest levels of the highest levels of comfort and adjustment creating the perfect fit, incorporating the latest fashion trends in every collection.

Our Fabrics have been carefully selected based on their quality, breathability and anti-allergic properties, choosing natural fibers such as bamboo, viscose or cotton. At design level, we have two main product lines: Print Line of trendy turbans & scarves and Comfort Line of turbans using smooth tissues.

Our Customers are our greatest source of inspiration. We strive on encouraging their femininity, elegance and individuality through our varied assortment of customizable models. Our creativity allows us to continuously explore new patterns and designs and further develop our growing palette of turbans that already counts on more than 70 models. Furthermore, new line of silk print scarves counts on 30 models in 3 different sizes.

Our Goal is to offer our customers a selection of products that reflect their character, style and life phase, and our wellness line is specially designed to help women preserve their beauty and confidence during their challenging times.

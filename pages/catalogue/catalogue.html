Let us introduce you to the world of BelleTurban!

Our turbans and scarves offer an effortless and simple way for expressing your individuality and unique fashion sense

Turban is linked intrinsically to the “ego” of a person and represents the prestige, while reflecting person’s character.

BelleTurban took this inspirational garment and further transformed it into a variety of fashionable statements, reflecting femininity, elegance and beauty.

Our turbans highlight women’s inner radiance, while preserving an element of mystery.

Our turbans and scarves are made to compliment your outfit and perfect it

We believe that every woman has her own approach to fashion. Even whilst somewhat conforming to the day’s trends, each one of us finds her own, unique way to personalise her look.

Colors of turbans convey mood

BelleTurban with its’ growing palette of garments, offers selection of styles and designs varying from simple plain color pieces to vivid colourful prints. Our customers are our greatest source of inspiration – we aim to offer them selection of turbans that reflect their individuality and distinctiveness.

BelleTurban is comfortable in any climate

Made from natural fibers, it is offering a necessary UV protection. At the same time, while worn in cold winter days, it offers a fashionable way of preserving warmth.
